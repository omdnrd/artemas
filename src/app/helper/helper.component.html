Help Page

Home

On the Home Page, you will see 5 boxes in the center named: Income, Expense, Family, Analysis, and Export. By clicking each individually, you will be brought to that selected page and will be able to track all of your income from the selected families, keep track of numerous expense categories, import active/inactive families, and view a total income/expenses.

Income

On the Income Page, you we be able to select the families you added from the 'Family Tab' in the given family drop-down box. From there, you can input their given income you recieved as well as the date you received it on. By hitting 'Enter', you will see the updated income category at the bottom of your screen.

Expense

On the Expense Page, very simliar to the Income Page, you will be able to select from different expense categories in the given drop-down menu: Classroom supplies, food, utilities, outdoor equipment, office supplies, building, and other. From there, you will be able to input the expense amount, choose the specific date of purchase, and a description of the expense. Once you hit 'Enter', you will see the updated expense category at the bottom of your screen.

Family

On the Family Page, this is where you will be able to input the active Family for that enrollment period. Once you hit 'Add Family', you will see the updated family category at the bottom of your screen. By hitting the 'View Families' button, you will be taken to a seperate page allowing you to view all families.

Analysis

On the Analysis, this is where you will see all of the running totals of Income and Expenses, and see which family has given payment.

Export

On the Export Page, you will be able to generate downloadable invoices through the given buttons; 'Generate Invoice' and 'Generate CSV'.
